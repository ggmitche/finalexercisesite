Pullover

The pullover is a fun, moderate strength exercise performed in calisthenics and gymnastics. This movement combines hanging leg lifts and pull-ups in order to get one's body over the top of a pull-up bar. It works the abdominal muscles, shoulders, forearms, lats, biceps, and forearms. It is also a great way to reach the top of the bar for straight bar dips (given enough space) if you aren't strong enough to do muscle-ups and don't have access to lower bars.

Insructions

  1. Begin by suspending yourself from the pull-up bar in a shoulder-width dead hang with palms facing outward
  2. Holding your body as high as you can, raise your legs up and over the bar
  3. As your legs come down and your upper body moves upwards, press out with your arms until your elbows are straight
  4. Reverse the motion and lower yourself back into a dead hang

Skin The Cat

Skin the cat is a popular intermediate exercise in both calisthenics and gymnastics training. It is used primarily to build strength and mobility in the shoulder girdle. It also builds strength in the abdominal muscles, the chest, the lats and the long head of the triceps depending on the position of your palms (pronated or overhand, and supinated or underhand). This exercise can be performed from either a pull up bar or with a set of gymnastic rings. With patience and proper progression, you can greatly increase shoulder mobility and make them highly resistant to injury.

Insructions

  1. Begin by suspending yourself from the pull up bar or rings in a dead hang position with your palms facing inward or outward.
  2. Squeeze your abs together and lift your legs to pull your body into an L-shape
  3. Continue by lifting your legs until your feet pass through the bar or rings
  4. Continue the movement until your legs are below your head and your feet are pointing towards the ground with your arms fully extended
  5. Reverse the movement by lifting the hips and raising your legs back and over until you return to the starting position/li>

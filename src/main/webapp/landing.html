Welcome to my app

Fitness can be either a state of health and well-being, or the ability to perform aspects of sports, occupations and daily activities. It can also be defined as one's ability to execute daily activities with optimal performance, endurance, and strength with the management of disease, fatigue, and stress and reduced sedentary behavior.

Regardless of occupation or background, the average person knows fitness has something to do with regular activity, proper nutrition, and healthy habits that form a solid foundation for quality living.
